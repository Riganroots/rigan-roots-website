Our Story

Born from a love for Nepal and a belief that travel should be meaningful, responsible, and deeply human.

Welcome to Rigan Roots & Routes

Rigan Roots & Routes was founded with a simple belief: that travel is most powerful when it connects people — not just to places, but to cultures, stories, and everyday life.

Nepal has always been more than mountains. It's a country of incredible diversity, warmth, and depth. Yet many travelers only scratch the surface. We're here to change that.

Every journey we craft is designed to go deeper — whether it's sharing a meal with a local family, walking ancient trails, or discovering a remote village that few visitors ever see.
