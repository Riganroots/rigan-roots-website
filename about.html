About Nepal

Nepal is not just a destination—it's an experience. Nestled between India and China, this Himalayan nation is a land of breathtaking landscapes, deep-rooted traditions, and warm, welcoming people. From the world's highest peaks to ancient temples and vibrant villages, Nepal offers a journey unlike anywhere else.

Country Overview

Capital: Kathmandu
Population: ~30 million
Known for: Himalayas, heritage, and hospitality

People & Culture

Nepal is home to over 125 ethnic groups and 123 languages. Each community carries its own traditions, festivals, and stories—making every journey a cultural discovery.

Nature & Landscapes

From the heights of Mount Everest to serene lakes, forests, and jungles, Nepal's natural beauty is both powerful and peaceful—perfect for adventure and reflection.
