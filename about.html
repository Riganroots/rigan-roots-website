Our Story

Four siblings, one passion — sharing the real Nepal with the world

Welcome to Rigan Roots & Routes

We're Ayush, Asmita, Aryan & Sweta — four siblings who grew up exploring every corner of our beautiful country. From the highest peaks to the deepest valleys, we discovered that Nepal has so much more to offer than what most travelers experience.

We created Rigan Roots & Routes to share the Nepal we know and love — a land of incredible diversity, warm hospitality, and life-changing experiences. Our mission is simple: to make travel in Nepal smoother, smarter, and more meaningful, while showcasing the true beauty of our culture and communities.

Every journey we design is personal, authentic, and from the heart. When you travel with us, you're not just a customer — you're family.
