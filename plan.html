Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a visa for Nepal? +

Yes, most nationalities can get a visa on arrival at the airport. Tourist visas are available for 15, 30, or 90 days.

Is it safe to trek alone? +

It's recommended to hire a licensed guide or join a group for safety, especially on high-altitude treks.

What currency is used? +

Nepalese Rupee (NPR). ATMs available in cities. Carry cash for remote areas.
