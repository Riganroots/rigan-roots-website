📅 Best Time to Visit

Autumn (Sept-Nov): Clear skies, perfect for trekking
Spring (Mar-May): Flowers bloom, pleasant weather
Winter (Dec-Feb): Cold but clear, fewer tourists
Monsoon (Jun-Aug): Lush greenery, low season prices

💰 Budget Guide (per day)

Backpacker: $20-30
Mid-range: $40-60
Comfortable: $70-100
Luxury: $150+

📋 Essential Tips

✓ Visa on arrival for most nationalities
✓ Travel insurance recommended
✓ Pack layers for changing weather
✓ Respect local customs
✓ Drink bottled/filtered water
