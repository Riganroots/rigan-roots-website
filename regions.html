Explore Nepal

Explore the Many Faces of Nepal

From sacred cities and timeless villages to remote valleys, hidden lakes, and Himalayan trails, discover the regions that make Nepal unforgettable.

Choose Your Nepal

Every region tells a different story. Some invite adventure, some offer stillness, and others open the door to culture, spirituality, and everyday life. At Rigan, we help travelers go beyond the obvious and discover Nepal with depth.

Kathmandu Valley

The cultural soul of Nepal, where ancient temples, living heritage, hidden courtyards, and vibrant Newari traditions shape every street and square.

🏛️ UNESCO 🍲 Newari Food 🏯 Heritage

Pokhara & Annapurna

A gateway to the Annapurna region, blending peaceful lakes, mountain reflections, soft adventure, village trails, and unforgettable trekking journeys.

🏔️ Trekking 🪂 Adventure 🌊 Lakeside

Everest Region

A legendary Himalayan world of Sherpa culture, dramatic alpine landscapes, ancient monasteries, and the iconic route toward Everest Base Camp.

🗻 Everest 🥾 Trekking 🕉️ Monasteries

Chitwan National Park

Nepal's wildlife heartland, where jungle safaris, rare rhinos, river landscapes, and rich Tharu culture reveal a completely different side of the country.

🐘 Safari 🦏 Rhinos 🎭 Tharu Culture

Eastern Nepal

A lesser-known region of rolling tea gardens, mountain communities, forested hills, and landscapes shaped by Limbu, Rai, and Himalayan traditions.

🍃 Tea Estates 🗻 Eastern Himalaya 🌿 Culture

Lumbini

The birthplace of Buddha, offering a peaceful spiritual atmosphere, international monasteries, sacred gardens, and journeys rooted in reflection and history.

🕯️ Buddha 🛕 Monasteries 🧘 Spiritual

Bandipur

A beautifully preserved hilltop town where Newari heritage, quiet stone lanes, mountain panoramas, and slow travel come together with elegance.

🏡 Homestays 🌄 Views 🏘️ Heritage Town

Upper Mustang

A remote Himalayan kingdom of desert-like cliffs, Tibetan Buddhist heritage, walled settlements, and rare cultural depth beyond the usual tourist path.

🏯 Hidden Kingdom 🕉️ Tibetan Culture 🪨 Dramatic Landscapes

Rara Lake

A remote alpine escape in western Nepal, known for still blue water, pine forests, quiet trails, and the rare feeling of complete Himalayan calm.

💙 Hidden Lake 🌲 Remote Nature 🏕️ Offbeat

Shey Phoksundo

A striking Dolpo landscape of turquoise lake views, high mountain wilderness, deep valleys, and one of Nepal's most remote adventure experiences.

💎 Blue Lake 🥾 Dolpo ⛰️ Remote Trek

Tsum Valley

A hidden Himalayan valley near Manaslu, where Buddhist culture, quiet monasteries, stone villages, and sacred mountain scenery remain beautifully preserved.

🙏 Sacred Valley 🏔️ Hidden Himalaya 🛕 Monasteries

Khaptad

A hidden plateau of rolling grasslands, forests, quiet trails, and spiritual stillness in far-western Nepal—ideal for travelers seeking peace and nature.

🌿 Meadows 🦜 Birdwatching ⛺ Camping

Panch Pokhari

A rewarding offbeat trek to five sacred alpine lakes, offering highland solitude, raw landscapes, and a spiritual atmosphere above the hills of central Nepal.

💧 Sacred Lakes 🥾 Trekking 🕊️ Peaceful

Ilam

A soft, green landscape of tea gardens, misty hills, local villages, and eastern calm—perfect for slow journeys, photography, and cultural escape.

🍃 Tea Country 🌫️ Misty Hills 📷 Scenic Escape

Dhorpatan

A rugged and rarely explored mountain region of alpine meadows, traditional settlements, and wide Himalayan silence far from the crowded trails.

🏔️ Remote Trails 🌾 Alpine Meadows 🧭 Hidden Nepal

Beyond the Popular Trail

Nepal is far more than a list of famous stops. Some of its most unforgettable experiences are found in remote valleys, hidden lakes, quiet hill towns, and lesser-known cultural landscapes. Rigan helps travelers discover both the iconic and the unseen.
