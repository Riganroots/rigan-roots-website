Explore the Many Faces of Nepal

From sacred cities and timeless villages to untouched valleys, hidden lakes, remote highlands, and Himalayan trails, discover the regions that make Nepal unforgettable.

Iconic Nepal Kathmandu, Pokhara, Everest, Chitwan, Lumbini, and Annapurna classics.

Choose Your Nepal

Every region tells a different story. Some invite adventure, some offer stillness, and others open the door to culture, spirituality, and everyday life. At Rigan, we help travelers go beyond the obvious and discover Nepal with depth.

Classic & Iconic Regions

Famous Nepal Experiences

These are the regions most international travelers already know, but Rigan can present them with deeper local connection.

☸

Kathmandu Valley

The cultural soul of Nepal, where ancient temples, living heritage, hidden courtyards, and vibrant Newari traditions shape every street and square.

🏛️ UNESCO🍲 Newari Food🏯 Heritage
✦

Pokhara & Annapurna

A gateway to the Annapurna region, blending peaceful lakes, mountain reflections, soft adventure, village trails, and unforgettable trekking journeys.

🏔️ Trekking🪂 Adventure🌊 Lakeside
☸

Everest Region

A legendary Himalayan world of Sherpa culture, dramatic alpine landscapes, ancient monasteries, and the iconic route toward Everest Base Camp.

🗻 Everest🥾 Trekking🕉️ Monasteries
✦

Chitwan National Park

Nepal's wildlife heartland, where jungle safaris, rare rhinos, river landscapes, and rich Tharu culture reveal a completely different side of the country.

🐘 Safari🦏 Rhinos🎭 Tharu Culture
☸

Lumbini

The birthplace of Buddha, offering a peaceful spiritual atmosphere, international monasteries, sacred gardens, and journeys rooted in reflection and history.

🕯️ Buddha🛕 Monasteries🧘 Spiritual
✦

Bandipur

A beautifully preserved hilltop town where Newari heritage, quiet stone lanes, mountain panoramas, and slow travel come together with elegance.

Untouched But Famous

Hidden Regions We Can Promote Strongly

These places are known among Nepali travelers and adventure lovers, but still feel raw, local, and less commercial for international guests.

Untouched Nepal✦

Kanchenjunga Region

Remote eastern Himalaya with huge mountain views, Limbu and Rai culture, cardamom villages, forest trails, and one of Nepal’s wildest trekking moods.

🏔️ Kanchenjunga🌿 Eastern Culture🥾 Remote Trek
High Value☸

Upper Mustang

A remote Himalayan kingdom of desert-like cliffs, Tibetan Buddhist heritage, walled settlements, sky caves, and rare cultural depth beyond the usual path.

🏯 Hidden Kingdom🕉️ Tibetan Culture🪨 Dramatic Landscapes
Far-West Gem✦

Rara Lake & Murma Top

A remote alpine escape in western Nepal, known for still blue water, pine forests, quiet trails, Murma Top viewpoint, and Himalayan calm.

💙 Hidden Lake🌲 Remote Nature🌄 Murma Top
Dolpo☸

Shey Phoksundo & Dolpo

A striking Dolpo landscape of turquoise lake views, Bon culture, high mountain wilderness, deep valleys, and one of Nepal's most remote experiences.

💎 Blue Lake🥾 Dolpo⛰️ Remote Trek
Sacred Valley☸

Tsum Valley

A hidden Himalayan valley near Manaslu, where Buddhist culture, quiet monasteries, stone villages, and sacred mountain scenery remain beautifully preserved.

🙏 Sacred Valley🏔️ Hidden Himalaya🛕 Monasteries
Peace Escape✦

Khaptad National Park

A hidden plateau of rolling grasslands, forests, quiet trails, spiritual stillness, wildflowers, and far-western hospitality.

🌿 Meadows🦜 Birdwatching⛺ Camping
Sacred Lakes☸

Panch Pokhari

A rewarding offbeat trek to five sacred alpine lakes, offering highland solitude, raw landscapes, and a spiritual atmosphere above central Nepal.

💧 Sacred Lakes🥾 Trekking🕊️ Peaceful
Hidden Nepal✦

Dhorpatan & Baglung

A rugged and rarely explored mountain region of alpine meadows, hunting reserve landscapes, Magar culture, traditional villages, and wide Himalayan silence.

🏔️ Remote Trails🌾 Alpine Meadows🧭 Hidden Nepal
Village Trails✦

Ruby Valley

A beautiful hidden trail between Langtang and Manaslu, known for Tamang and Gurung villages, quiet ridges, local homestays, and raw Himalayan scenery.

🏡 Homestays💎 Hidden Trail🥾 Soft Trek
Wild East✦

Makalu Barun

A wild eastern Himalayan region with deep valleys, forests, waterfalls, remote settlements, and dramatic views near the world’s fifth-highest mountain.

🏔️ Makalu🌳 Wilderness🥾 Expedition Feel
Community Travel✦

Numbur Cheese Circuit

An underrated trail in Ramechhap with alpine lakes, yak pastures, cheese-making stories, Sherpa culture, and fewer crowds than famous trekking routes.

🧀 Cheese Trail🏔️ Alpine Lakes🏡 Local Villages
Lowland Wild✦

Bardiya National Park

A wilder and quieter alternative to Chitwan, ideal for tiger tracking, river landscapes, Tharu culture, birdwatching, and deep jungle experiences.

🐅 Tiger Tracking🌿 Jungle🎭 Tharu Culture
Slow Travel✦

Ilam & Mai Pokhari

A soft, green landscape of tea gardens, misty hills, sacred wetlands, local villages, and eastern calm—perfect for photography and slow travel.

🍃 Tea Country🌫️ Misty Hills📷 Scenic Escape
Ancient City✦

Tansen Palpa & Rani Mahal

A romantic hill-town region with old streets, Newari influence, Magar culture, sunrise views, and the riverside beauty of Nepal’s Rani Mahal.

🏰 Rani Mahal🏘️ Hill Town🌄 Sunrise
Mithila Culture☸

Janakpur & Mithila Region

A colorful cultural region of Janaki Mandir, Mithila art, sacred ponds, festivals, sweet food, and a completely different rhythm of Nepal.

🎨 Mithila Art🛕 Janaki Mandir🎉 Festivals
River Culture✦

Koshi Tappu

A peaceful wetland region for birdwatching, river life, village culture, buffalo landscapes, and slower nature experiences in eastern Terai.

🦜 Birdwatching🌊 Wetlands🚣 River Life
Far-West Culture✦

Api Nampa & Darchula

A far-western Himalayan region with remote valleys, borderland culture, high peaks, traditional life, and powerful adventure potential.

🏔️ Api Himal🧭 Far-West🏡 Local Life
Village Escape✦

Ghalegaun & Ghanpokhara

A warm village region known for Gurung culture, homestays, mountain views, traditional food, dances, and genuine people-to-people travel.

🏡 Homestay🎭 Gurung Culture🍚 Local Food

Beyond the Popular Trail

Rigan can promote these regions through emotional storytelling: local hosts, food, festivals, homestays, village skills, spiritual sites, nature walks, and human connection.
