Questions

Host Questions

Do I need to speak English?

No, perfect English is not required. Basic communication is helpful, and Rigan can support guest coordination.

Do I need a big house?

No. A clean, safe, welcoming home is more important than size or luxury.

Can I host only food experiences?

Yes. You can offer cooking, lunch, dinner, momo making, dal bhat, or local snack experiences.

Can I choose my available dates?

Yes. Hosts can decide when they are available for guests.

How do I get paid?

Rigan helps manage bookings and payment support professionally after each confirmed experience.
